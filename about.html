My Story

My name is Sannidhya Sandheer. I am a computer engineer(to be), studying at (not) one of the finest institutions in India; NMIMS, Shirpur.

This is (obviously) my blog. The blog is an outlet for ideas. It is also a subtle attempt at a conversation that I hope you, my dear visitor, engage me in.

I am always quite intrigued by Scandinavian culture. The name of the blog, 'Leita', is an Icelandic verb that means 'to search'. In a way, this blog is my search for something, which I find hard to define at the moment. It is also, a documentation of this very vague search.
